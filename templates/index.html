Usage

The current API supports 2 endpoints: one for the direction and one for the speed.

  • Direction endpoint, supports the following parameters:
    • 'forward': moves the car forward
    • 'reverse': moves the car in reverse
    • 'left': turns the car to the left
    • 'right': turns the car to the right
  • Speed endpoint, supports the following parameters:
    • 'slow': slow speed
    • 'medium': medium speed
    • 'fast': fast speed
    • 'stop': stops the car
